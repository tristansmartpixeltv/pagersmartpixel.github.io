Real Estate

Real Estate

A bird eye view of the project in its urban surroundings.A user-interface with the buttons and menus to access the different sections and functionalities of the software. Since it’s a first experience for most users, the interface is completely intuitive and user-friendly.The user can rotate around the project and zoom in and out by moving their fingers on the 3D environment while easily access any sections and features. Touching the building will automatically zoom on the project and activate the unit selection tool.

SEARCH AND SELECTION FILTERS

Easily find the right property by selecting different criterias such as the surface, price range, number of rooms or availability. The software will highlight the units corresponding to the selected criterias with colored semi-transparent 3D volumes. Touching the highlighted units will either show the floorplans section or open the information window of the corresponding unit. A quick selection tool will also highlight a unit by simply entering its number.

FLOOR PLANS

The floorplan view will cut the building to the selected floor allowing to see each units represented by a colored volume. Fully 3D divided and furnished floor plans are offered as an option. User can browse through different floors to compare. Touching a unit or a space will open a pop-up window with the corresponding unit information.

UNIT INFORMATION AND UNIT PLANS

Information about units are usually displayed in a pop-up “floating” window with the unit number, characteristics, surface, price and any other useful information. The unit information window contains different buttons to open the plan, print, access the panoramic view, customize the unit, send the information by email etc. Multiple information windows and unit plans can be opened at the same time for an easy comparison.

PANORAMAS

What is the view from windows and balconies is a recurring question, especially for high rise projects. The panoramas allows the user to see the panoramic view from any unit and use the “time slider” to displace the sun and see the actual lighting on different time of the day or night. Beside the interactive 3D skyline, we can add actual pictures and drone views in option.

COMMON AREAS AND AMENITIES

Common areas and amenities are always a key feature of real estate projects. This section of the software will one one hand show a general perspective of the project to easily locate all the common areas with clear labels, and on the other hand, let users zoom in a fully immersive view.

SURROUNDINGS

The neighbors and lifestyle are another important aspect of the project. This section will bring the view to a higher bird eye view so the user can see all nearby attractions. Points of interests will usually be divided into general categories such as transportation, health, food and drink, education or entertainment, each category having it’s specific color code. Interactive labels floats above each location. Touching it will display related content and description. The optional “travel to” feature will enable to zoom and rotate around a point of interest.

TIME SLIDER

By simply sliding the finger on the timeline, the user can displace the sun in it’s real course to any time of the day, from sunrise to sunset. We take a special attention in producing impressive vividly colored sunrises and sunsets and in the animation of lights and shadows. The night environment is offered in option.

EMAIL AND CRM TOOL

This tool allows sending users information about selected units (plans, documents, images, technical details or pricing). When linked to your CRM, this will also create the customer entry and assign the record to proper internal user (sales representative or other employee).

ONLINE CONTENT MANAGER AND CONNECTIVITY

Beside a realtime connectivity with any CRM, ERP or asset management software, every SmartPixel.tv software comes with an online Content Management System enabling users to manage and update the content if the application at a distance.

MULTIPLATFORM

Any SmartPixel.tv software can be declined on multiple platforms beside PC/touchscreens version (the ideal experience for sales or leasing offices). Use a mobile/tablet either in Android, iOs or Windows version to travel and attend meetings, deploy the interface on your website with a realtime 3D WebGL version, display on giant videowalls or use the latest virtual, augmented or mixed reality headsets to enhance the experience.
